Personicle 2.0

Personicle is a Mobile App which in its current form collects and process the location, place, mobile device specific measures like Calendar, Ambient Light, phone usage, and measures like Heart Rate, Activity Level, and Sleep from wearable devices like Fitbit or smart watches. The first release uses Android phones and Fitbit device; soon iOS and other smart wearables will be used. Currently, data is collected at every 5-minute interval to create activities and events, that maybe used by health systems.

Currently I am working on version 2.0 of the app. I'll be using IOT and AWS technology to help detect basic actions and parse the information to send a signal to the user in case of any potential health problems.
