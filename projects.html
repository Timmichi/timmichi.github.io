Projects

Here are a few things I've created. Whenever I want to make something, I map out a requirements model and use case. Although my projects are relatively small scale, I want to create things that will eventually be used by people. My goal is to make my projects feel less like projects and more like polished, clean, and accessible software.

Treat Yo'Self

A budgeting web application that entices new budgeters with its clean UI/UX and an easy to use dashboard, Treat Yo'Self is what our next generation of budgeters needs. Designed in Figma and developed with a MongoDB database, Django backend, and React frontend, this is my first attempt at a full-stack web application.
